Privacy Policy

Tempo Controller (Video Speed Controller) does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal or sensitive user data.

Data Storage

All settings, such as selected playback speed, toggle state (enabled/disabled), and badge position, are stored locally in the user's browser using Chrome local storage.

Data Access

The extension does not read website content, track browsing activity, or access personal information.

Third-Party Services

No analytics, advertisements, or third-party services are used. Everything runs locally within the browser.
